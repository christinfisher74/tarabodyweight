How to run it
40 seconds of work, 20 seconds of rest, per move. Run each circuit 3 rounds before moving to the next. Rest 60–90 sec between circuits.

Daily Mobility Flow
Run through this anytime — before training, after a long day, or as its own short session. About 8–10 minutes total.
90/90 Hip Switch1 min/side
Seated, rotate through both hips slowly. Stay tall through the spine, no rushing the transition.
World's Greatest Stretch5/side
Lunge, drop the back knee, rotate up toward the front leg's sky. Opens hips, chest, and t-spine in one move.
Cat-Cow8 reps
Slow, full range through the spine. Inhale as you arch, exhale as you round.
Standing Quad Pull30 sec/side
Hold onto a wall if balance is tricky. Knee points straight down, gentle pull — never forced.
Shoulder CARs5/side
Slow controlled circles, full range. Keep the rest of the body still — isolate the shoulder.
Deep Squat Hold1 min
Hold at the bottom, gently rock side to side. Use a doorframe for support if needed.
Thread the Needle5/side
From hands and knees, thread one arm under and through. Slow rotation through the upper back.
